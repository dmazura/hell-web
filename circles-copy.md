## Limbo
**Limbo** — A place of endless waiting where nothing hurts enough to end and nothing matters enough to begin.

## Lust
**Lust** — Desire loops endlessly, promising intimacy and satisfaction while delivering only hunger without fulfillment.

## Gluttony
**Gluttony** — Consumption continues long after pleasure is gone, turning excess into exhaustion and need into punishment.

## Greed
**Greed** — Possession becomes burden as nothing can be released and worth is measured only by accumulation.

## Wrath
**Wrath** — Anger feeds on reaction, escalating endlessly without resolution, direction, or relief.

## Heresy
**Heresy** — Truth is delivered fully formed, unquestioned and closed, replacing thought with certainty.

## Violence
**Violence** — Order is maintained through force, where suffering is normalized and obedience replaces will.

## Fraud
**Fraud** — Identity is endlessly optimized for approval, hiding reality behind polished illusions that never satisfy.

## Treachery
**Treachery** — Trust is performed publicly while betrayal is prepared in silence, freezing all genuine connection.
